The Framework
The 3-Step Story Framework
Every feature you demo has a story hiding inside it. Here's how to find it and tell it in a way that makes prospects lean in.
1
The Setup
2
The Shift
3
The Payoff
Step 1 — The Setup
Paint the “Before” World
Describe the painful reality your prospect lives in right now. Use specifics they recognise: the spreadsheet chaos, the 3am phone calls, the revenue leaking through the cracks.
Example: “Picture this: it's Monday morning and your planner has 40 orders to schedule. They're juggling three spreadsheets, calling drivers on WhatsApp, and manually checking which trailer is at which depot. By 10am they've already made two mistakes that will cost real money.”
Step 2 — The Shift
Introduce the Change
This is where Qargo enters the story — not as a product pitch, but as the turning point. Show the moment everything changes. Keep it brief, specific, and visual.
Example: “Now, that same planner opens Qargo. Every order is already there, auto-imported from their customers' systems. The optimiser has grouped loads by route and matched them to available drivers. Instead of building the puzzle from scratch, they're reviewing a plan that's already 90% done.”
Step 3 — The Payoff
Reveal the “After” World
Paint the outcome — not in feature language, but in the customer's language. Think: time saved, stress reduced, revenue gained, errors eliminated.
Example: “By 9:15, planning is done. The planner spends the rest of their morning handling exceptions instead of building plans. They go home on time. Drivers know their schedules before they've finished breakfast. And finance can see exactly what every load is costing in real time.”
Comparison
Feature Dumping vs. Storytelling
Feature Dump

“Qargo has automated planning, real-time visibility, driver app integration, CMR generation, subcontractor management, and API connections.”

Story

“One of our customers used to spend two hours every morning just building their plan. Three weeks after going live with Qargo, planning takes 15 minutes.”

Cheatsheet
Story Starters by Feature
Ready-to-use Before → Shift → After scaffolds you can adapt for your next call.
Story Starter
Automated Planning & Optimisation
Before
Planners spending 2+ hours manually assigning orders. Constant re-planning as drivers call in sick or loads change. Revenue lost to empty kilometres.
Shift
Qargo's optimiser builds the plan in minutes, factoring in vehicle type, driver hours, location, and delivery windows automatically.
After
Planning in 15 minutes. 12% fewer empty kilometres. Planners now focus on exceptions and customer relationships instead of puzzle-solving.
Story Starter
Driver App & Real-time Visibility
Before
Operations managers fielding dozens of “where's my truck?” calls daily. Drivers using WhatsApp for PODs. No audit trail. Customers frustrated.
Shift
Every driver has real-time updates on their phone. PODs are captured digitally and attached to the order instantly. Customers get live tracking links.
After
80% fewer status calls. PODs available within minutes of delivery. Customer satisfaction scores up. Operations team reclaims hours every week.
Story Starter
Automated Documentation (CMR/ePOD)
Before
Admin teams buried in paper CMRs. Missing documents delaying invoicing by days. Disputes unresolvable because nobody can find the original paperwork.
Shift
Qargo generates CMRs automatically and captures electronic proof of delivery. Everything is linked, searchable, and archived in one place.
After
Invoicing within 24 hours of delivery. Disputes resolved in minutes with full digital audit trail. Admin team freed up to focus on growth.
Story Starter
Integrations & API
Before
Orders arriving by email, phone, and portal. Someone manually re-keying every order into the TMS. Errors creeping in. Customers sending the same info twice.
Shift
Qargo connects directly to customer systems, ERPs, and partner platforms. Orders flow in automatically with zero manual entry.
After
Zero re-keying errors. Orders appear in Qargo the moment they're created. Everyone works from one source of truth.
Story Starter
Subcontractor Management
Before
Subcontractor rates tracked in spreadsheets. No visibility on whether outsourced loads are profitable. Disputes about agreed prices. Chaotic self-billing.
Shift
Qargo stores rate cards, auto-calculates costs, and gives real-time margin visibility on every subcontracted load. Digital acceptance and self-billing built in.
After
Full margin visibility per load, per sub. Rate disputes eliminated. Self-billing automated. Finance knows exactly what's owed before the month is closed.
The Golden Rule
Features Tell. Stories Sell.
Every time you demo a feature, wrap it in a Before → Shift → After story and watch your prospects lean in.
