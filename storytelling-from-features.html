The 3-Step Story Framework

Every feature you demo has a story hiding inside it. Here's how to find it and tell it in a way that makes prospects lean in.

1

The Setup Paint the "Before" World

Describe the painful reality your prospect lives in right now. Use specifics they recognise: the spreadsheet chaos, the 3am phone calls, the revenue leaking through the cracks. Make them nod.

Qargo Example

"Picture this: it's Monday morning and your planner has 40 orders to schedule. They're juggling three spreadsheets, calling drivers on WhatsApp, and manually checking which trailer is at which depot. By 10am they've already made two mistakes that will cost real money."

2

The Shift Introduce the Change

This is where Qargo enters the story — not as a product pitch, but as the turning point. Show the moment everything changes. Keep it brief, specific, and visual.

Qargo Example

"Now, that same planner opens Qargo. Every order is already there, auto-imported from their customers' systems. The optimiser has grouped loads by route and matched them to available drivers. Instead of building the puzzle from scratch, they're reviewing a plan that's already 90% done."

3

The Payoff Reveal the "After" World

Paint the outcome — not in feature language, but in the customer's language. Think: time saved, stress reduced, revenue gained, errors eliminated. Make the future feel real and achievable.

Qargo Example

"By 9:15, planning is done. The planner spends the rest of their morning handling exceptions instead of building plans. They go home on time. Drivers know their schedules before they've finished breakfast. And finance? They can see exactly what every load is costing in real time."

Feature Dumping vs. Storytelling

Feature Dump

"Qargo has automated planning, real-time visibility, driver app integration, CMR generation, subcontractor management, and API connections. You can also configure custom workflows and set up automated notifications."

Story

"One of our customers used to spend two hours every morning just building their plan. Their planner was so stressed she was thinking about quitting. Three weeks after going live with Qargo, planning takes 15 minutes. She told us she finally has time to actually manage exceptions instead of just reacting to them."

Automated Planning & Optimisation

+
Before

Planners spending 2+ hours manually assigning orders. Constant re-planning as drivers call in sick or loads change. Revenue lost to empty kilometres.

Shift

Qargo's optimiser builds the plan in minutes, factoring in vehicle type, driver hours, location, and delivery windows automatically.

After

Planning in 15 minutes. 12% fewer empty kilometres. Planners now focus on exceptions and customer relationships instead of puzzle-solving.

📱

Driver App & Real-time Visibility

+
Before

Operations managers fielding dozens of "where's my truck?" calls daily. Drivers using WhatsApp for PODs. No audit trail. Customers frustrated by lack of updates.

Shift

Every driver has real-time updates on their phone. PODs are captured digitally and attached to the order instantly. Customers get live tracking links.

After

80% fewer "where's my truck?" calls. PODs available within minutes of delivery. Customer satisfaction scores up. Operations team reclaims hours every week.

📄

Automated Documentation (CMR/ePOD)

+
Before

Admin teams buried in paper CMRs. Missing documents delaying invoicing by days. Disputes unresolvable because nobody can find the original paperwork.

Shift

Qargo generates CMRs automatically and captures electronic proof of delivery. Everything is linked, searchable, and archived in one place.

After

Invoicing within 24 hours of delivery. Disputes resolved in minutes with full digital audit trail. Admin team freed up to focus on growth.

🔗

Integrations & API

+
Before

Orders arriving by email, phone, and portal. Someone manually re-keying every order into the TMS. Errors creeping in. Customers sending the same information twice.

Shift

Qargo connects directly to customer systems, ERPs, and partner platforms. Orders flow in automatically with zero manual entry.

After

Zero re-keying errors. Orders appear in Qargo the moment they're created. Customers see live status updates in their own systems. Everyone works from one source of truth.

💰

Subcontractor Management

+
Before

Subcontractor rates tracked in spreadsheets. No visibility on whether outsourced loads are profitable. Disputes about agreed prices. Chaotic self-billing process.

Shift

Qargo stores rate cards, auto-calculates costs, and gives real-time margin visibility on every subcontracted load. Digital acceptance and self-billing built in.

After

Full margin visibility per load, per sub. Rate disputes eliminated. Self-billing automated. Finance team knows exactly what's owed, to whom, before the month is closed.

Remember the Golden Rule

Features tell. Stories sell. Every time you demo a feature, wrap it in a Before → Shift → After story and watch your prospects lean in.
