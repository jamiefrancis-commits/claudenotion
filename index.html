Key Players
Who's Involved?
Network Operator
Runs the hub, sets standards, manages IT, handles billing. e.g. Palletways, Pall-Ex, Palletline.
Member Hauliers
Independent companies covering local postcodes. They collect, trunk, and deliver. Boots on the ground.
Shippers / Customers
Businesses sending palletised goods. Book through a member for nationwide delivery at a fraction of the cost.
